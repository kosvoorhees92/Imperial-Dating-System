How Imperial Time Works

Imperial Date is based on the "Anno Domini" system, but adapted for the Warhammer 40,000 universe. It consists of three parts:

  • Year Fraction: The year is divided into 1000 parts (000-999), each representing approximately 8.76 hours.
  • Year: The last three digits of the year (e.g., 023 for 2023).
  • Millennium: The current millennium (e.g., M3 for the 3rd millennium).

Imperial Time is measured within the current Year Fraction. Each Year Fraction is divided into:

  • Hours: 0-8 (since each fraction is ~8.76 hours).
  • Minutes: 0-59.
  • Seconds: 0-59.
